5.3. Implicit fileset plus mapper on additional input port

  1 <calabash includes="doc.xml"
  2           inPort="source"
              destdir="out"
  4           extension=".compare.xml"
              pipeline="compare-001.xpl">
  6     <input port="alternate">
            <globmapper from="*.xml" to="*-alt.xml" />
  8     </input>
    </calabash>

5.4. Fixed input, multiple mapped outputs

  1 <calabash in="group-003_input1.xml" pipeline="group-003_pipeline.xpl">
  2     <output port="result">
            <globmapper from="*_input1.xml" to="out/*_result.xml" />
  4     </output>
        <output port="result2">
  6         <globmapper from="*_input1.xml" to="out/*_result2.xml" />
        </output>
  8 </calabash>

5.5. QNames

  1 <calabash in="in.xml" out="out.xml" pipeline="pipeline.xpl">
  2     <namespace prefix="hi" uri="low" />
        <parameter name="{hi}there" value="a value" />
  4     <parameter name="hi:there" value="a value" />
    </calabash>

{hi}there is a QName in Clark notation, where hi is the namespace URI and there is the local name; whereas hi in hi:there is a namespace prefix that is bound to the low namespace URI.
